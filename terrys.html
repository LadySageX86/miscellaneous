Taxes and Import Charges

As we are a business based in Nova Scotia, all purchases made within Canada will be charged our sales tax, with the exception of our jewellery listings, which include taxes. In addition, there may be import charges involved with international purchases, so please be aware of these before making purchases, and be sure to message us if you have any questions or concerns.

Shipping and Handling

We do our best to ship things out the same day they're purchased, but as we're operating from a physical storefront, this cannot always be the case. Please allow up to three days for shipping before contacting us. Any purchases made on a Sunday will be shipped the following Monday.
